Breath

This project focuses on the breath, known in the Eastern world as the first natural truth conditioning the well-being and functionality of the body.

Adjusting the breath can alter the body's alchemy within seconds. Breath() aims to provide a comprehensive understanding of how this chemical exchange occurs 30,000 times a day and its effects on the body and health.
We study the intimate connection between what we breathe, how we breathe, and how we feel, as well as the deeper unconscious layers that guide and are guided by this process.

Utilising a vast database of syntactic data and studying countries Air Quality Index (AQI) and its limitations (often only reflecting the highest index value and ignoring other factors), Breath() addresses the economic rather than health-driven factors that have made air pollution a silent killer, responsible for more deaths than smoking.

Our complex algorithm weighs, normalises, processes, and outputs the true AQI and its effects on the body, aiming to shed light on these issues and guide users toward addressing this silent killer.
What and how you Breathe will condition how you feel.
